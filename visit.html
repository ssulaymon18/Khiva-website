Best Time to Visit

  • 🌸 Spring: Mild weather, gardens
  • 🍂 Autumn: Perfect temps, golden
  • ☀️ Summer: Hot, less crowded
  • ❄️ Winter: Magical, few tourists
✈️

Getting There

  • ✈️ Fly to Urgench (UGC)
  • 🚗 30-min drive to Khiva
  • 🚂 Overnight train from Tashkent
  • 🚌 Shared taxis from cities
🏨

Where to Stay

  • 🏛️ Inside Itchan Kala
  • 🛏️ Guesthouses with locals
  • 🏨 Modern hotels near center
  • ⛺ Desert yurts (unique!)
🍛

Must-Try Food

  • 🥘 Plov (rice with meat)
  • 🥟 Manti (steamed dumplings)
  • 🍲 Shurpa (meat soup)
  • 🍬 Halva & green tea
💡

Local Tips

  • 📱 Offline maps
  • 🗣️ Learn local phrases
  • 📸 Ask before photos
  • 🎁 Haggle respectfully
  • 🕌 Dress modestly
  • 💧 Bottled water

🏛️ Top Things to Do in Khiva

Must-do activities for your visit

Explore Itchan Kala

Walk through the UNESCO-listed inner city with 50+ monuments and ancient streets.

Climb the Minarets

Get panoramic views from Islam Khodja and Kalta Minor minarets.

Shop at the Bazaars

Discover local crafts, silk carpets, ceramics, and spices in the old markets.

Watch the Sunset

Enjoy golden-hour views from the city walls with the desert beyond.
