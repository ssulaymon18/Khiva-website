Best Time to Visit

  • 🌸 Spring: Mild weather, gardens
  • 🍂 Autumn: Perfect temps, golden
  • ☀️ Summer: Hot, less crowded
  • ❄️ Winter: Magical, few tourists
✈️

Getting There

  • ✈️ Fly to Urgench (UGC)
  • 🚗 30-min drive to Khiva
  • 🚂 Overnight train from Tashkent
  • 🚌 Shared taxis from cities
🏨

Where to Stay

  • 🏛️ Inside Itchan Kala
  • 🛏️ Guesthouses with locals
  • 🏨 Modern hotels near center
  • ⛺ Desert yurts (unique!)
🍛

Must-Try Food

  • 🥘 Plov (rice with meat)
  • 🥟 Manti (steamed dumplings)
  • 🍲 Shurpa (meat soup)
  • 🍬 Halva & green tea
💡

Local Tips

  • 📱 Offline maps
  • 🗣️ Learn local phrases
  • 📸 Ask before photos
  • 🎁 Haggle respectfully
  • 🕌 Dress modestly
  • 💧 Bottled water
